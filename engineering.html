Engineering

Join our Program in Civil and Electrical Engineering, designed to prepare you for a high-tech, global career at the forefront of Industry 4.0. With 1 year in India and 3 years in Germany.

Why Choose This Program?

Year 1 in India:
  • Master the German language ( B2).
  • Gain essential soft skills for communication, collaboration, and leadership.
  • Build a solid foundation in civil and electrical engineering basics.
Years 2-3-4 in Europe:
  • Experience dual education, with practical, on-the-job training.
  • Collaborate with world-renowned engineering companies
  • Earn a monthly stipend of €1,400–€1,500 while training X 36 months in Europe.

Program Highlights:

  • Length: 4 years (1 in India + 3 in Europe)
  • Diploma: Recognised globally!
  • Government-Sponsored Tuition in Germany
  • Stipend: Earn €1,400–€1,500/month X 36 months = €50,400 in Europe

What Makes This Program Special?

  • Learn from the Best: Train with top-notch engineering firms and institutes in Europe.
  • No Blocked Account Required: Hassle-free admission process.
  • Study Gap Accepted: Flexibility for a fresh start.
  • Guaranteed Career Opportunities: Build a secure, well-paying career in engineering.
